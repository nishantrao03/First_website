About XYZ

XYZ is a cultural event taking place usually in the month of September or October every year.

XYZ 2022 is the 5th edition of the annual cultural event XYZ. Due to the prevailing pandemic situation in the last two years, the event was held in online mode. But this year, we are glad to announce that XYZ will be held completely offline.
